Master Craftsmanship Since 1999

For over 25 years, MasterCraft has been the trusted choice of artists and galleries worldwide, delivering precision-engineered stretcher bars that preserve and showcase fine art for generations.

Our Story

MasterCraft began in a small woodworking shop with a simple mission: create the finest stretcher bars that artists and galleries could trust with their most valuable works. What started as a passion project has grown into the industry's gold standard for canvas support systems.

Our founder, master craftsman Robert Mitchell, believed that every artwork deserved a foundation as carefully crafted as the piece itself. This philosophy drives everything we do, from selecting premium kiln-dried lumber to engineering our patented keyable corner system.

Today, MasterCraft stretcher bars support artworks in prestigious galleries, museums, and private collections around the world. We're proud to be the silent partner behind countless masterpieces, providing the stable foundation that allows art to speak for generations.

Precision Craftsmanship

Every MasterCraft stretcher bar undergoes a meticulous 12-step process that ensures gallery-quality results and lasting performance.

Premium Lumber Selection

We source only the finest kiln-dried pine, oak, and poplar from sustainable forests, ensuring each piece meets our strict quality standards for grain, moisture content, and structural integrity.

Precision Milling

Our state-of-the-art milling equipment creates exact profiles with tolerances within 0.1mm, ensuring perfect joints and consistent dimensions across every piece we produce.

Hand-Finished Edges

Each stretcher bar receives meticulous hand-sanding and edge profiling, creating the smooth, rounded edges that prevent canvas damage and ensure professional stretching results.

Engineered Corner Joints

Our patented keyable corner system allows for precise tension adjustment over time, while our fixed joints use reinforced mortise and tenon construction for maximum stability.

Quality Assurance

Every stretcher bar undergoes rigorous quality control, including moisture content testing, dimensional verification, and structural integrity checks before receiving our MasterCraft seal of approval.

Professional Packaging

Each order is carefully packaged with protective materials and moisture barriers, ensuring your stretcher bars arrive in perfect condition, ready for immediate use.

Our Journey

From a small family workshop to an internationally recognized brand, our commitment to quality has never wavered.

1999

Foundation

Robert Mitchell establishes MasterCraft in his garage workshop, focusing on custom stretcher bars for local artists and galleries.

2003

Innovation

Development of our patented keyable corner system, revolutionizing canvas tension adjustment for professional artists.

2008

Expansion

Move to our current 15,000 sq ft facility, equipped with state-of-the-art machinery and quality control systems.

2015

Recognition

Selected as the official stretcher bar supplier for major museum exhibitions and international art fairs.

2024

Legacy
